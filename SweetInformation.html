Sweet Harmony Gardens



Taytay, Rizal


CELEBRATE EVENTS IN A BEAUTIFUL DESTINATION VENUE

Located East of Manila, Sweet Harmony Gardens is approximately 2 hectare garden combining the beauty of nature and urban pleasure. Choose from its seven lovely venues, with different thematic ambiance including a three story mansion with different banquet halls, making it the biggest events place in its vicinity.

Surrounded by lush greens, the whole venue has a collection of hundred fruit trees, a stretch of tropical plants and an array of blooming flowers that are highlighted with thousands of vivid lights. Adorned with very unique eco-friendly chandeliers that are made with plastic bottles that brings additional glare when touched by lights that gives drama to their venues. Also noted for its 85 ft. rainbow-lighted Tree of Hope that serves as additional attraction at night.

Sweet Harmony Gardens is the perfect events venue destination from your intimate gatherings, birthday parties to elegant wedding ceremonies. With its diverse venue options combined with their offerings, you are guaranteed to have your dream event in Sweet Harmony Gardens.

Organizing an event has never been that easy, especially on getting the best venue for your special occasions. But with Sweet Harmony Gardens getting affordable yet elegant events venues won't be a problem anymore. With their Sunrise Deal you can have your morning events the way you want, may it be for weddings, baptism, birthdays or even reunions for they have a lot of spacious venues.
