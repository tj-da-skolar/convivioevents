About Us


Thinking for your own upcoming grand celebration might be exciting, but planning for it can be exhaustive. Worry no more because we have just made it easier with Convivio!

Convivio is a web-based application made for people who are planning for events like debut and wedding. Convivio makes it possible for users to customize the package that they want to have for their event. The user can choose what type of services to include in their package, choose from a couple of our partners and book the date.



Our Mission


To make the process of booking events with event services companies more convenient for those who are planning to have them by creating their own package.



Our Vision


Our vision is to reduce the amount of stress in the overall process of event management through the use of Convivio.



Company Info


Everything started in September 2014, when a female Computer Science student from De La Salle University - Manila realized the difficulty of planning for a grand celebration, such as 18th birthday debut, based from her own experience with the process of organizing an event for her special day way back.

She started to form a group composed of students with same course and university as her that would make her idea happen.
