Mixed Mobile Bar



Leon Guinto St, Malate, Metro Manila


MIxed Mobile Bar, under the operations of Mixed Drink Nation Co., is the leading
Mobile Bar in the Philippines. Formed in 2008, we offer alcoholic and non - alcoholic
concoctions on all kinds of events. We catered to more than a thousand events - debuts,
weddings, parties, company events etc. - all throughout Metro Manila and all over Luzon.
Currently, Mixed Mobile Bar has the capability to accomodate events with up to 3000 guests
and can cater to 10 small events per day. We are composed of 25 team members, all of whom
are professionals from HRM, Catering Industry, Restaurant Industry etc. The Company expects
to grow more in the next years.

In Mixed Mobile Bar, we strive to exceed the expectatios of our customers. We believe that
team work and excellent customer service are significant in delivering an outstanding service.
We treat our customers with honesty, respect and right consideration.
