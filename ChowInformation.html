Chow Unlimited Corporation



Ortigas Center, Pasig City


Chow Unlimited Corporation was founded on March 2001 with the goal to provide
our customers delectable food paired with excellent service at reasonable prices.
Today, we remain true to this commitment, continually aiming to improve so that we
can serve you better.

From business seminars to special events like kiddie parties, debut and wedding reception,
let our experienced staff handle it to suit any of your catering needs.
We also have two function rooms and two al fresco garden, ideal for business or private functions.

CHOW UNLIMITED - FUNCTION ROOMS

For in-house catering, we have two fully air-conditioned function rooms located at
the roof deck of Prestige Tower, right at the heart of Ortigas Central Business District.

FUNCTION ROOMS

Function Room 2 can accommodate up to 100 people with a rate of P2,000/hour.
Function Room 2 with partial extension to Function Room 1 can accommodate up to 150 people, with a rate of 3,000/hour
Function Room 2 with extension to Function Room 1 can accommodate up to 250 people, with a rate of 4,000/hour.
Function Room 3 can accommodate up to 70 people with a rate of P1500/hour.

Amenities:
tables and chairs
mantle and tablecloths
flower arrangement for the buffet table
centerpieces for each table
stage
overhead screen
podium
sound system (amplifier, speakers, microphone)

To avail free use of our function room for 4 hours, the client must have at least P30,000
(for Function Room 2) or P15,000 (for Function Room 3) worth of food catering booking
(applicable only to adult menu and exclusive for 10% service charge). In excess of 4 hours,
standard rates will apply. Prices are subject to change without prior notice.
