One Cafe and Events Place



Ortigas Center, Pasig City


One Cafe and Events Place aims to be the premier provider of catering and food service needs of various companies and organizations. Boasting of collective experiences in the restaurant industry, the One Cafe and Events Place team promises to meet and exceed the demands of our prospective clients. The core is formed by Chefs, Restaurant Managers and Sales Experts from service industry premier restaurants. Service Oriented, Pristine Execution and Value for Money. These are the core values that the group strongly lives by. We guarantee delectable International cuisine with a twist inventive interpretations and modern creations turning the very best cuisine traditions into exciting and unique experience guaranteed to tempt your palate and thrill your guests. One Cafe and Events Place provides full service catering, party planning and event management from corporate receptions to weddings, and fundraisers to backyard barbeques. We can custom-design each event to ensure a spectacular experience. With your demands in all aspects of the food business, One Cafe and Events Place is full of vibrant ideas to cater to your NEEDS flexibility & sufficient sources to reach the demands for SATISFACTIONS. One Cafe and Events Place is the perfect solution for corporate events, receptions and parties. And because our menu is as varied as it is exciting, you can tailor your event to be the exact experience you want. Please call us to customize a menu. Located at the 6th floor of the One Corporate Centre along Julia Vargas Corner Meralco in the heart of the Ortigas Central Business District.
