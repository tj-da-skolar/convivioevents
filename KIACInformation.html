KIAC Cakes and Cupcakes



Taytay, Rizal


KIAC Cakes & Cupcakes was established in 2011 by Joanna Orteza & her partner Carmela Fuentes. Created from their combined passion for cooking and baking, they were able to form Keyks in A Cup. A home based business in Taytay, Rizal that started offering scrumptious cupcakes as giveaways and supplying establishments. The partnership decided to focus on making their own circle of market, and with the growing market in social media, they were able to make online pages in Facebook, as well as Instagram. Shortly afterwards, Keyks in A Cup changed their names to KIAC Cakes and Cupcakes upon offering customization of cakes and signature cakes.

KIAC Cakes and Cupcakes offers a wide variety of both classic and innovative flavors to customers of all ages. They also offer a wide range of cakes which can be customized to the customer's tast. All of this is because KIAC Cakes and Cupcakes sells great flavors in small packages.

KIAC Cakes and Cupcakes is located in #12 Downhill Street, Barangay Dolores, Towerhills Subdivision, Taytay, Rizal.
